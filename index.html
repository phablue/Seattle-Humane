Volunteer With Us

Humane Volunteer

Volunteers are critical to our mission and are at the heart of all we do. They outnumber our staff by nearly 20-to-one and contribute to every department in our organization.

Humane Teen Club

This club is for teens ages 13-17 interested in animal welfare and becoming volunteers at Seattle Humane before they turn 18.

Help Our Campaign

Animal People Can

We’re in the midst of a $25 million fundraising campaign to build a first-in-the-region animal shelter, adoption center, and teaching hospital.
This new facility will replace our aging shelter facilities on Seattle’s Eastside, which currently limit our capacity to save more animals and place them into loving homes.
